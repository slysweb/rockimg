How to Crop GIFs Online

Trim GIF edges, focus on specific areas, and reduce file size with a free online GIF cropper.

Published on: Jan 27, 2026

Why Crop GIFs?

Cropping GIFs helps you remove unwanted edges, focus on the important part of the animation, and often reduces file size. A smaller GIF loads faster on websites and social media.

Key Features

  • Animated preview so you can see the full GIF before cropping.
  • Drag to select the area you want to keep.
  • Maintains quality - cropped GIFs keep the same animation quality.
  • Browser-based - all processing happens locally, no uploads to servers.
  • Instant download - get your cropped GIF immediately.

Step-by-Step: Crop a GIF

Step 1: Upload Your GIF

Drag and drop your animated GIF file or click to select. The tool supports standard animated GIF format.

Step 2: Preview the Animation

Watch the GIF play in the preview area. This helps you decide which area to keep.

Step 3: Select the Crop Area

Drag to select the rectangular area you want to keep. You can adjust the selection box to frame the perfect area.

Step 4: Crop and Download

Click Crop to process the animation. The cropped GIF maintains all frames and animation quality. Download when ready.

Tip: Cropping removes pixels from the edges, which can reduce file size. Smaller GIFs load faster on social media and websites.

Best Practices

  • Crop tightly around the main subject to reduce file size.
  • Keep important action in the center of the crop area.
  • Test different crop sizes to balance file size and composition.
  • Use cropping to remove watermarks or unwanted borders.

Conclusion

Cropping GIFs online is quick and easy. Upload, select your area, crop, and download a smaller, focused animation.
